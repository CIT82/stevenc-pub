Demarice Rice #24

Demarice Rice has emerged as a dynamic and talented quarterback for the Groundhogs over the past two years. Known for his exceptional skills, leadership on the field, and dedication to the game, Rice has become the face of the team's offensive prowess.

Total Career Statistics:

Touchdown Passes: 65
Completion Percentage: 66.5%
Rushing Yards: 900 yards
Rushing Touchdowns: 12

Demarice Rice's burgeoning career promises continued success and excitement for Groundhogs fans. With a remarkable start, Rice is on the path to becoming a franchise quarterback and a symbol of excellence for the Groundhogs organization for years to come.
