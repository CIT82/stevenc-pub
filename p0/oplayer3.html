Jack Capaldi #12

Jack Capaldi, the star wide receiver for the Groundhogs, has electrified fans with his exceptional athleticism and a penchant for making game-changing receptions. Known for his ability to come through in clutch situations, Capaldi has become a reliable go-to target and a vital asset to the team's offensive success over his four-year career.

Total Career Statistics:

Receptions: 265
Receiving Yards: 4,200 yards
Touchdowns: 39
Game-Winning Receptions: 11

As Jack Capaldi continues into his fourth season, his career statistics demonstrate his impact as a premier wide receiver. With a reputation for clutch performances and a consistently high level of play, Capaldi is expected to remain a key playmaker for the Groundhogs, contributing to their offensive success in future seasons.
