Ricky Parks #7

Ricky Parks, the explosive running back for the Groundhogs, has been a force on the field, dazzling fans and earning accolades throughout his career. With a dynamic playing style and a knack for finding the end zone, Parks has become a cornerstone of the Groundhogs' offense.

Total Career Statistics:

Rushing Yards: 4,500 yards
Rushing Touchdowns: 37
Receiving Yards: 1,200 yards
Receiving Touchdowns: 13
Total Yards from Scrimmage: 5,700 yards

Ricky Parks' career trajectory points towards continued success and a legacy as one of the Groundhogs' all-time greats. As the starting running back, Parks remains a key asset, bringing excitement and excellence to the team's offensive arsenal. Groundhog fans eagerly anticipate the thrilling performances and milestones that Ricky Parks will undoubtedly achieve in the seasons to come.
